Tourist Treasures

Grab your suitcase and take off to find Tourist Treasures! Match numbers, reveal the bonus and win prizes in this globe-trotting adventure!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each globe symbol in the LUCKY NUMBERS panel to reveal a number.

Press each suitcase symbol in the YOUR NUMBERS panel to reveal a number.

Match any LUCKY NUMBER to any identical YOUR NUMBER to win the corresponding prize.

Press the BONUS TILE for a chance to reveal an instant prize value.

When all the LUCKY NUMBERS, YOUR NUMBERS and the BONUS ticket are revealed, the game ends. A closing message then appears indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Press STOP to continue the game in manual play mode.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
